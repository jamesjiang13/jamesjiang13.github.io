About me

I am a software engineer with experience in Javascript, React, Redux, Node.js, Express, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and D3.js. While on a thought-provoking and reflective trip around the world, I saw that my passion was in helping people through technology. I latched on to this passion and upon my return, immediately worked to sharpen my skills as a full stack web developer. I find my joy in building expressive, engaging, and socially responsible applications to address the problems of the future; I look forward to expanding my skillset by working at a company and helping to build a product that I believe in.

In my spare time, I enjoy culturally immersive traveling to expand my understanding of the world. I also love trekking mountain ranges (Sierra Nevadas, Himalayas, Los Andes), practicing my Spanish at every embarrassing opportunity, and sharing positive vibes at latin dance events.
